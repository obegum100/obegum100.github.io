WHAT I DO

Passion for art and creativity, I love to design and code. With over 7 years of diversified experience in UI Design, Front-End Web Development and Software Testing. I enjoy working with clients to get the best results for their work and I love seeing that I was able to bring a mere thought and idea to life.

A graduate of Queens College, CUNY with a Bachelor's in Graphic Design and certification from General Assembly in Front-end Web Development. I hunger for innovation and love to create visual products that represent meaningful change in the community.

I’ve had a lot of jobs, a colorful career, and a passion for learning. I hope you enjoy viewing my work, if you do give me a follow.
